Naksan BBQ


Naksan BBQ in Arlington is your destination for an authentic Korean barbecue experience. Explore a menu that blends traditional grilling techniques with modern flair, featuring premium meats and flavorful side dishes. Enjoy tabletop grilling in a welcoming atmosphere that captures the essence of Korean culinary traditions. Immerse yourself in the unique fusion of tradition and innovation at Naksan BBQ.


Address:

1414 Commonwealth Ave, Boston, MA 02135


Operating Hours

Monday to Friday 05:00 PM - 12:00 AM

Saturday to Friday 12:00 PM - 12:00 AM

Gyu-Kaku Japanese BBQ


Gyu-Kaku Japanese BBQ in Boston offers a dynamic dining experience where guests can indulge in the art of tabletop grilling with a distinctive Japanese flair. The menu features high-quality meats, fresh vegetables, and a variety of marinades for a personalized barbecue adventure. Embrace the interactive and social aspect of Japanese barbecue in a vibrant atmosphere, making Gyu-Kaku a must-visit for those seeking a unique and delicious culinary journey in the heart of Boston.


Address:

23 District Ave, Dorchester, MA 02125


Operating Hours:

Monday to Saturday: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM

Sunday: 11:00 AM - 09:30 PM

Mahaniyom


Mahaniyom in Brookline, MA, is a cozy gem known for its craft cocktails and Thai sharing plates. Nestled in a compact, brick-lined space, the restaurant exudes a chill vibe, offering an intimate setting for guests to enjoy inventive drinks and flavorful Thai dishes in a relaxed atmosphere.


Address:

236 Washington St, Brookline, MA 02445
