OBOSA RESTAURANT

Obosa Restaurant in Boston is a flavorful journey to Nigeria. Imagine the best home-cooked Nigerian dishes served with a modern twist. Their menu

is a celebration of Nigerian cuisine, featuring classics like jollof rice and suya, each dish carefully prepared to bring out the authentic taste of Nigeria

FASIKA CAFE

Step into Fasika Cafe in Boston for an unforgettable journey into the heart of Ethiopian flavors, akin to

savoring home-cooked Ethiopian delights with a modern twist. With classics like injera and doro wat, each dish is crafted with precision

to evoke the true essence of Ethiopian cuisine. Whether you're already enchanted by the tastes of Ethiopia or curious to know more,

Fasika provides a cozy and inviting space to explore the bold and intricate flavors that define Ethiopian dining.


Address:

51 Roxbury St, Boston, MA 02119


Operating Hours

CESARIA KITCHEN

Cesaria Kitchen, a hidden gem in Boston, welcomes visitors to experience the spirit of Cape Verde through its vibrant dishes

The kitchen is a celebration of the diverse culinary tapestry that defines Cape Verdean cuisine, showcasing beloved classics like cachupa and pastel.

Guests can immerse themselves in the laid-back atmosphere, where every bite becomes a connection to the heart and soul of Cape Verde.


Address:

266 Bowdoin St, Boston, MA 02122


Operating Hours:

Sunday to Wednesday: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM

Thursday to Saturday: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM



Eat with Cesaria

RED-RED KITCHEN

In the heart of Boston, Red-Red Kitchen, a Ghanaian restaurant, caters to the city's palate through delivery, pickup, and catering options.

Patrons can indulge in the rich and diverse flavors of Ghanaian cuisine, relishing classics such as jollof rice and waakye. Enjoying these dishes in the

comfort of their homes, patrons are treated to an authentic taste of Ghana with each flavorful bite.


Address:

94 Shirley St, Boston, MA 02119

Monday - Tuesday, Thursday - Saturday: 11:00 AM - 4:00 AM

KARIBU RESTAURANT

In Boston, Karibu Restaurant warmly welcomes patrons for a dine-in experience that celebrates the vibrant flavors of Kenya.

Revel in the authenticity of Kenyan cuisine, with each bite of nyama choma and sukuma wiki bringing the rich culinary heritage of Kenya to life.

Seated in the cozy ambiance of the restaurant, every visit to Karibu is a flavorful journey through Kenya, where the warmth of hospitality and the

joy of Kenyan dining create an unforgettable experience.


Address:

10 Crescent St, Waltham, MA 02453
