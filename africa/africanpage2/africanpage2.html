AFRICAN RESTAURANTS

A Taste of Boston

SUYA JOINT

The Suya Joint is a Nigerian classic right here in Boston. It's a casual space of traditional Western African cooking and serves the best cocktails.

They also serve delicious vegan Nigerian meals, which is a rarity in many West-African meals. In your free time,

be sure to pass by the Suya Joint and have a grand time surrounded by flavorful dishes.


Address:

185 Dudley St, Boston, MA 02119


Operating Hours:

Sunday: 2:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Tuesday to Saturday: 4:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Closed on Mondays

ASHUR CAFE

For Halal African and Middle-Eastern food, Ashur Cafe is the spot to be. Ashur is a simple neighborhood eatery in the heart of Roxbury.

Along with traditional African/Middle-Eastern meals like Kebab and Biryani, amongst others, Ashur also served American dishes which evokes the true

essence of the African/Middle-Eastern immigrant experience. Whether you're a big fan of Middle Eastern and African dishes or want to know more,

Ashur Cafe will be pleased to host you.


Address:

291 Roxbury St, Roxbury, MA 02119


Operating Hours

Sunday to Saturday 7:00 AM - 11:00 PM

DAYIB CAFE

Dayib Cafe, one of the best East-African restaurants, welcomes visitors to experience the cordon bleu of Africa's East Coast!

With dishes spanning from lamb kobob to refreshing drinks like a Somali Ginger Tea that will warm you, Dayib celebrates East-African cuisine,

showcasing beloved classics from the motherland. Guests can sit-back, relax and enjoy more of Dayib and the beauty it is.


Address:

722 Shawmut Ave, Boston, MA 02119


Operating Hours:

Sunday to Friday: 11:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Saturday: 11:00 AM - 7:00 PM
